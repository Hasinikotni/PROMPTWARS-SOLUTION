# PromptWars Submission

## Challenge Title
Election Process Education

## Prompt
Act as an interactive AI assistant that teaches users about the election process in India. 
Explain step-by-step in simple language:
1. What is an election?
2. How candidates are selected
3. Voting process (online/offline)
4. Counting of votes
5. Declaration of results

Make it beginner-friendly, use real-life examples, simple analogies (like classroom elections), and include short quizzes after each section to make it interactive. Keep answers short and easy to understand.

## Output

### 1. What is an Election?
An election is a process where people choose their leaders by voting. For example, just like choosing a class leader in school, citizens vote to select representatives who make decisions for the country.

❓ Quiz: Why do we conduct elections?

---

### 2. How are Candidates Selected?
Candidates are chosen by political parties or can stand independently. Parties select people based on leadership qualities and public support.

❓ Quiz: Can a person contest elections without a party?

---

### 3. Voting Process
People vote either using Electronic Voting Machines (EVMs) or ballot papers. Voters go to polling booths and cast their vote secretly.

❓ Quiz: Why is voting kept secret?

---

### 4. Counting of Votes
After voting ends, all votes are counted carefully. EVMs are opened and votes are tallied to see who got the most votes.

❓ Quiz: Who ensures fair counting?

---

### 5. Declaration of Results
The candidate with the highest votes wins. The results are officially announced by the Election Commission.

❓ Quiz: What happens after results are declared?

---

## Explanation
This prompt was designed to make learning interactive by adding examples, simple language, and quizzes for better understanding.
